Design a modern, high-conversion, responsive website UI for a local dental clinic named:

SmileCare Dental Clinic

This website must be optimized for lead generation and appointment bookings.

Adults aged 25–50

Parents booking dental appointments for children

Patients looking for cosmetic dentistry

People with urgent dental pain

Clean and minimal

Plenty of white space

Soft blue and white color palette (medical trust colors)

Accent color for CTA buttons (teal or deep blue)

Use modern sans-serif typography

Rounded buttons and cards

Subtle shadows for depth

Clear visual hierarchy
